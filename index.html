About Me

My name is Shane Khant Soe Moe. I am from Yangon, Myanmar and currently residing in Maryland, USA. I went to the Montgomery College, Rockville for A.A degree in Computer Science and the University of Maryland, College Park (UMD) for B.Sc degree in Computer Science (major) and Statistics (minor). Currently, I am now working as an associate software engineer at Oil Price Information Service (OPIS by IHS Markit). My favorite programming language are C# & Python. I have experience in developing web applications (Django, MERN & .Net Core), cross-platform mobile applications (ReactNative), and IoT projects. I love the concept of the internet of things (IoT) and trying to learn development tech stacks, protocols and distributed systems about IoT. In my free time, I would like to play FIFA mobile game, chess or participate in Hackathons. My favorite football club team is Manchester United and national team is Germany (Yes, I am a football fan).
